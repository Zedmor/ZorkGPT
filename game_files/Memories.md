## Location: West of House (ID: 180)

## Location: Behind House (ID: 79)
**[SUCCESS - PERMANENT] Open window, enter** *(Ep1, T7-8, +10)*
Window is slightly ajar. Open it and enter to reach Kitchen. First 10 points.

## Location: Living Room (ID: 193)
**[SUCCESS - PERMANENT] Move rug, open trap door** *(Ep1, T14-15, +0)*
Rug hides a trap door. Moving rug then opening door reveals staircase to Cellar.
Trap door always bars itself when you go down - must reopen from above each time.
Trophy case is here - deposit treasures for points.

## Location: Kitchen (ID: 203)
**[SUCCESS - PERMANENT] Take sack and bottle** *(Ep1, T9-10, +0)*
Brown sack has lunch and garlic. Bottle has water. Chimney leads down to Studio (one way up only - can't climb down). Dark staircase leads up to Attic.

## Location: Attic (ID: 201)

## Location: Cellar (ID: 72)

## Location: Troll Room (ID: 102)

## Location: Loud Room (ID: 138)
**[SUCCESS - PERMANENT] Say "echo" then take platinum bar** *(Ep1, T66-67, +10)*

## Location: Dam (ID: 215)
**[SUCCESS - PERMANENT] Press yellow button in Maintenance, then turn bolt with wrench** *(Ep1, T47-50, +0)*

## Location: Entrance to Hades (ID: 232)
**[SUCCESS - PERMANENT] Bell/Book/Candles ritual** *(Ep1, T220-226, +0)*
1. Ring bell (drops and turns hot, candles fall out)
2. Light match, light candles with match (NOT torch - torch vaporizes them!)
3. Take candles (spirits cower)
4. Read book (spirits banished)
Key: Must be holding candles when reading book. Don't bring torch - it vaporizes candles.
